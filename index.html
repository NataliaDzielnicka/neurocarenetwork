NeuroCare Network

Our Mission

Providing Awareness Across South Florida

NeuroCare Network raises awareness about neurological disorders by uniting communities through acts of kindness and striving to build a supportive network for patients battling these conditions. Our mission is to make a positive impact on the lives of those affected by neurological disorders, contributing to a more compassionate and informed society.

The students tell Parkland Talk that the NeuroCare Network has made significant strides in its mission. During Autism Awareness Month, they hosted a painting event at Eagles Haven, where over 50 community members and students from Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School came together to create motivational and positive canvases for pediatric patients at Joe DiMaggio Children’s Hospital. The team also recently collaborated with various STEM, science, and service clubs at their school to create NeuroCare packages for those who need it most.
